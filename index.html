What We Offer

Centenary Hospital provides a comprehensive range of healthcare services designed to meet the diverse needs of our Canadian community. Our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced medical professionals ensure you receive the highest quality care across all departments.

Emergency Department

Our 24/7 emergency department is staffed with experienced physicians and nurses ready to handle urgent medical situations. From minor injuries to life-threatening conditions, we provide immediate, expert care when every minute counts.

Walk-in Clinic

No appointment necessary for non-urgent medical concerns. Our walk-in clinic offers convenient access to healthcare for routine check-ups, minor illnesses, and preventive care services during extended hours.

Diagnostic Imaging

Advanced imaging services including X-ray, ultrasound, CT scans, and MRI. Our certified technologists use cutting-edge equipment to provide accurate diagnostic results to support your treatment plan.

Laboratory Services

Comprehensive lab testing with rapid turnaround times. From routine blood work to specialized testing, our accredited laboratory ensures accurate results for effective diagnosis and treatment monitoring.

Specialist Referral

Seamless connections to specialist care when needed. Our referral system ensures continuity of care and timely access to specialized medical expertise across various disciplines.

Patient Services

Comprehensive support services including patient advocacy, discharge planning, and care coordination to ensure your healthcare journey is smooth and well-coordinated from admission to recovery.

How It Works

Getting the care you need at Centenary Hospital is straightforward and efficient. Our streamlined processes ensure you receive timely attention while maintaining the highest standards of medical care and patient safety.

1

Arrival & Registration

Present your health card and ID at registration. Our staff will verify your information and create or update your patient record in our secure system.

2

Triage & Assessment

For emergency visits, our triage nurse will assess your condition and prioritize care based on medical urgency. For scheduled appointments, you'll be directed to the appropriate department.

3

Medical Care

Receive expert medical attention from our qualified healthcare professionals. We'll explain your condition, treatment options, and answer any questions you may have.

4

Follow-up & Discharge

Before leaving, you'll receive clear instructions for follow-up care, prescriptions if needed, and any necessary referrals to specialists or additional services.
