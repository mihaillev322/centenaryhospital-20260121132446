About Centenary Hospital

Discover our commitment to excellence in healthcare, our dedicated team of professionals, and the values that guide our mission to serve the Canadian community with compassionate, quality medical care.

Our Story

Centenary Hospital serves Canadian patients with a focus on timely, compassionate care across emergency, outpatient, and diagnostic services. Built through partnerships with local clinicians and community organizations, we aim to improve access to coordinated care and clear patient information. Our mission is to support patients and families with safe care pathways, respectful communication, and continuous quality improvement.

Since our establishment, we have grown to become a cornerstone of healthcare in our community, expanding our services and capabilities to meet the evolving needs of Canadian families. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare that treats every patient with dignity and respect. Through decades of service, we have built lasting relationships with patients, families, and healthcare partners across the region.

Our commitment extends beyond medical treatment to encompass the entire patient experience. We understand that healthcare can be overwhelming, which is why we've developed comprehensive support systems, clear communication protocols, and patient-centered policies that prioritize your comfort and understanding. Every decision we make is guided by our dedication to improving health outcomes and enhancing the quality of life for those we serve.

Today, Centenary Hospital stands as a testament to the power of collaborative healthcare, bringing together skilled professionals, advanced technology, and community partnerships to deliver exceptional medical services. We continue to evolve and adapt, embracing innovation while maintaining the personal touch and compassionate care that defines our identity as a trusted healthcare provider in Canada.

Our Values

Our core values shape every aspect of our healthcare delivery and guide our interactions with patients, families, and the broader community. These principles are not just words on a wall - they are the foundation of our daily practice and decision-making processes.

Compassionate Care

We treat every patient with empathy, kindness, and understanding, recognizing that behind every medical condition is a person with unique needs, concerns, and hopes. Our compassionate approach extends to families and caregivers, ensuring everyone feels supported throughout their healthcare journey.

Clinical Excellence

We are committed to the highest standards of medical practice, continuously updating our knowledge, skills, and technologies to provide the most effective treatments available. Our pursuit of excellence drives us to participate in ongoing education, research, and quality improvement initiatives.

Patient Safety

Safety is our top priority in every procedure, interaction, and decision. We maintain rigorous safety protocols, invest in advanced safety technologies, and foster a culture where every team member is empowered to speak up about potential safety concerns without fear of retribution.

Respect and Dignity

We honor the inherent worth of every individual, respecting diverse backgrounds, beliefs, and values. Our inclusive environment ensures that all patients receive culturally sensitive care that acknowledges their unique perspectives and preferences in healthcare decisions.

Transparency

We believe in open, honest communication with patients and families about diagnoses, treatment options, risks, and outcomes. Our commitment to transparency extends to our policies, procedures, and quality metrics, fostering trust through clear and accessible information.

Community Partnership

We actively collaborate with community organizations, healthcare partners, and local leaders to address broader health challenges and improve overall community wellness. Our partnerships extend our impact beyond our walls to create healthier communities across Canada.

Our Team

Centenary Hospital's strength lies in our diverse team of healthcare professionals who bring together decades of experience, specialized training, and an unwavering commitment to patient care. Our multidisciplinary approach ensures that every patient receives comprehensive, coordinated care from admission through recovery.

Medical Staff

Our physicians represent a wide range of medical specialties, each board-certified and committed to evidence-based practice. From emergency medicine specialists who provide life-saving care around the clock to family physicians who focus on preventive care and chronic disease management, our medical staff brings expertise and compassion to every patient encounter. Many of our physicians also participate in medical education and research, ensuring that our practices reflect the latest advances in medical science.

Nursing Excellence

Our nursing team forms the backbone of patient care at Centenary Hospital. Led by experienced nurse managers and clinical nurse specialists, our registered nurses, licensed practical nurses, and nursing assistants provide 24/7 bedside care with skill and compassion. Our nurses participate in ongoing professional development, specialty certifications, and evidence-based practice initiatives to ensure the highest quality of patient care.

Allied Health Professionals

Our comprehensive care team includes pharmacists, respiratory therapists, medical laboratory technologists, diagnostic imaging technicians, physiotherapists, occupational therapists, social workers, and dietitians. These professionals work collaboratively to address all aspects of patient health and recovery, ensuring that care plans are holistic and tailored to individual patient needs.

Support Services

Behind every successful patient outcome is a dedicated team of support professionals including patient registration staff, medical records personnel, housekeeping, food services, security, and administrative staff. These team members ensure that our hospital operates smoothly and efficiently, creating a safe, clean, and welcoming environment for patients and visitors.

Contact Information

We're here to help you navigate your healthcare needs. Whether you have questions about our services, need to schedule an appointment, or require assistance with any aspect of your care, our dedicated staff is ready to assist you.

General Information

For general inquiries, appointment scheduling, and information about our services, our main reception desk is staffed by knowledgeable personnel who can direct you to the appropriate department or provide immediate assistance.

  • Main Phone: Available 24/7 for urgent inquiries
  • Business Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Weekend Support: Saturday-Sunday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Emergency Services

For life-threatening emergencies, call 911 immediately or proceed directly to our Emergency Department. Our emergency team is available 24/7 to provide immediate, life-saving care when you need it most.

  • Emergency Department: 24/7 availability
  • Trauma Services: Level II trauma center
  • Ambulance Access: Direct emergency entrance

Patient Services

Our patient services team is dedicated to ensuring your hospital experience is as smooth and comfortable as possible. We can assist with billing questions, insurance matters, and any concerns about your care.

  • Patient Advocacy: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Billing Inquiries: Monday-Friday, 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Medical Records: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Specialized Services

For appointments with specialists, diagnostic imaging, laboratory services, or other specialized care, our dedicated booking coordinators will help you schedule appointments and provide preparation instructions.

  • Specialist Referrals: Processing within 48 hours
  • Diagnostic Booking: Same-day and next-day availability
  • Lab Services: Walk-in and appointment options
